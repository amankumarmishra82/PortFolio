Project Overview

The E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard is an end-to-end data analytics project developed to analyze retail sales performance and generate actionable business insights. The analysis was performed on a retail dataset containing 9,994 transactions, helping transform raw business data into meaningful insights through interactive dashboards and visualizations.

Problem Statement

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day. However, without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify top-performing products, profitable customer segments, regional sales trends, and loss-making areas. The objective of this project is to convert raw sales data into business intelligence that supports data-driven decision making.

Project Objectives

  • Analyze overall sales and profitability performance.
  • Identify top-performing and loss-making products.
  • Evaluate customer segment contribution.
  • Discover regional sales and profit trends.
  • Build an interactive dashboard.
  • Generate actionable business recommendations.

Dataset Information

  • Dataset: Superstore Sales Dataset
  • Records: 9,994 Transactions
  • Customers: 793 Unique Customers
  • Orders: 5,009 Orders
  • Categories: Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology
  • Regions: East, West, Central, South

Key Business Insights

Product Analysis

  • Technology generated the highest sales and profit.
  • Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier was the highest revenue-generating product.
  • Several products generated negative profit despite high sales.

Customer Analysis

  • Consumer Segment contributed the highest revenue.
  • Tamara Chand was the most profitable customer.
  • Some customers generated high sales but low profitability.

Regional Analysis

  • West Region delivered highest profit.
  • California was the best-performing state.
  • Texas generated high revenue but significant losses.

Business Performance

  • Generated over $2.3M in sales.
  • Achieved $286K profit.
  • Profit margin reached 12.47%.

Business Recommendations

  1. Increase investment in Technology products.
  2. Review discount strategies in loss-making states.
  3. Retain high-profit customers through loyalty programs.
  4. Investigate loss-making products.
  5. Replicate successful strategies from California and West Region.

Project Outcome

This project successfully transformed raw retail sales data into actionable business intelligence using SQL and Power BI. The analysis provided valuable insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional trends.
